01
We hear our client’s needs and convert them into feature requests, which are then divided into smaller tasks, and define their levels of complexity. We maintain separate feature branches on Github to ensure code segregation & consistency. Simultaneously, we keep fixing any issues which may arise within these feature requests.
02
Once done, we allow our QA Testers to assess if the feature works as expected & our clients can also review them simultaneously if needed. If any issues are surfaced, we go back to fix them.
03
In the last stage, our reviewer looks at the code for any further improvements or corrections and once its found to be meeting our client’s expectations, we make the changes live on their store.
